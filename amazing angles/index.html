Welcome to Amazing Angles School

PRINCIPAL: MS.NARGIS SHEIKH

Guided by our esteemed Principal, Ms. Nargis Sheikh, Amazing Angels aims to be a leader in education, not just in Khajrana but beyond. Ms. Sheikh's vision and leadership inspire our community to continually adapt to emerging educational trends and uphold the highest standards of excellence.

In summary, Amazing Angels Public School is more than an educational institution; it is a dynamic community committed to nurturing minds, fostering growth, and preparing students to shine in a world of limitless possibilities. Join us in this enriching journey of learning, discovery, and success.

Our Vision

At our school, we strive to cultivate a lifelong passion for learning, combining quality education with character development. Our state-of-the-art facilities create an environment that nurtures every student.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our academic achievements and the numerous achievements of our students in sports and other activities, building a strong reputation in the city and state.

Welcome to Amazing Angles

At Amazing Angels Public School, our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, holistic development, and values-driven education. We aim to inspire a passion for learning, instill a sense of curiosity, and equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for success in an ever-evolving world. Our commitment extends beyond the classroom, as we endeavor to cultivate responsible, compassionate, and resilient individuals, ready to contribute positively to society.
